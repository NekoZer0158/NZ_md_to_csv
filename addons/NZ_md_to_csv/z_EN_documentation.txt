How to use:
	1) Create a resource UpdateCSV_resource.
	2) Fill main_folder_path, output_folder_path, folders and languages.
	3) Open UpdateCSV dock.
	4) Drag and drop your resource in that dock.
	5) Press the "Update" button; after that, csv files will appear in the folder with the path you have written in output_folder_path.

Debug - needed for testing

!IMPORTANT! 
Import of csv files might not happen; if so, just start and close the project (F5,F8)
